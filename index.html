Minimal composition for photo and moving image.

Built as a quiet white room where each frame carries weight, with enough breathing space to feel like a gallery wall.

The work sits between stillness and motion. Light, texture, and pacing are treated as the main subjects.

Less decoration, more presence. The page acts like an exhibition room so every image and video can stand on its own.
